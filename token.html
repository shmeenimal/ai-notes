Token

The basic unit of text that LLMs process. Neither a character nor a full word — a chunk somewhere in between.

  • ~0.75 words per token on average in English (100 tokens ≈ 75 words)
  • Common words are usually 1 token; rare or long words split into several
  • Misspelled words tokenize inefficiently — the tokenizer doesn't recognize them and breaks them into smaller, less meaningful fragments, costing extra tokens
  • Non-Latin scripts (Arabic, Chinese, Hindi) are less token-efficient than English
  • Tokens are the unit of both cost and memory in every LLM system
